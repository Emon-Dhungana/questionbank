Inserting a Question

  1. Questions cannot be inserted without completely selecting the values of semester, subject, chapter, and difficulty level from dropdowns and filling the fields.

Using Icons

Icons are used for navigation and actions:

  1. Home: Go to home page
  2. Question Retrieve
  3. Date
  4. Dark Mode
  5. Log out

Retrieveing a Question

To retrieve questions,

  1. Navigate to the Question Retrieve
  2. Questions will be displayed after clicking the filter button

Deleting a Question

To delete a question,

  1. Navigate to the Question Retrieve
  2. Questions will be displayed after clicking the filter button. From there, questions in the database can be updated and deleted.

Updating a Question

To update a question,

  1. Display questions by navigating to the Question Retrieve page and clicking the filter button.
  2. Click on the Update button next to the question you want to update. This will take you to another page where you can edit the question.
  3. After making the desired changes, click on the Update button again.
  4. Click the Filter button to see the updated question.
